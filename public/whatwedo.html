What we do

Working with farmers and producer organisations, we ensure you get the best produce and they get the best price. All the produce are organically grown. When it reaches you, you get the information which tells you what came from where. We thus empower you with the knowledge of how your produce is grown.

Our motto is simple - make accessible the food which is safe, nutritious and fresh to everybody. That's why we just don't deliver fruits and vegetables but also this critical information.

Who are we

Among us are experienced technologists, farmers and enthusiasts who are solving some of the most critical problems related to our food. With the help of open long indigenous traditional knowledge and new digital innovations, we are working towards creating a sustainable ecosystem which benefits all.

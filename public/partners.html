Udbhavam Farms

A homemaker when made her garden an urban farm realized that she needed to do more. For past three years, Rakhi Mittal has been doing organic farming, teaching the farmers and motivating them to adopt sustainable practices. She runs a school for kids from nearby villages at her farm and treats her farm like a temple.

She manages a 1.5 acre farm in Noida. She grows seasonal vegetables, grains, herbs and flowers. She has indigenous cows and thus uses cow dung and urine to prepare compost and natural pest repellent along with other natural ingridents like agricultural waste and neem leaves.

Desi Utpad by Jaya

Jaya and Rajesh Mishra are farmers who have been growing organically for past 10 years. They grow on their own 2 acres of under-certification land in Saharanpur and also have leased out farms in Tehri Garhwal area for long term. They work with various organic farmers and Self Help Groups in Himachal Pradesh and continously are getting associated with more farmers from other areas as well. They are collectively managing more than 40 acres of farms spread across various regions.

They grow seasonal as well as exotic varieties of vegetables, fruits and grains. They follow only organic practices of farming and believes in soil rejuvenation.

Nisarg Farms

Born in the farmers' family, Rajnesh is now a passionate organic farmer who has been growing organically from past 1 year. Out of the 8 acres of leased land in Ghaziabad, he has been growing seasonal vegetables on 2 acres of it. He now has an indigenous cow and uses cow dung and urine to prepare Jivamrita, Neemastra and compost. He collects kitchen waste and agricultural waste to prepare compost and thus increasing soil fertility. He also uses yellow traps and ash to repel pests.

He learns better practfices of farming by reading and watching on Internet. He communicates with us using WhatsApp, navigates using Maps and keeps accounts of all the activities and expenses of the farm. He now knows both English as well as Hindi names of vegetables. He is the first person in his family who has a Savings Bank Account.
